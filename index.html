Our Love Story

August 2016 — Nicholas and Ashley met, and from that very first moment, something special began. What started as friendship quickly revealed itself to be something far deeper; an undeniable love neither of them could ignore.

On September 15, 2017, after asking more than once, Nicholas finally asked Ashley in the simplest way: “Yes or no?” And with that, she said yes to a love that would change their lives forever; a moment they still hold close to their hearts.

From that day forward, their journey has been filled with love, growth, and unwavering devotion. Through every season of life, they have stood side by side, building a bond that has only grown stronger with time.

On August 23, 2025, they began counting down the days to forever knowing their greatest chapter was just ahead. Now, surrounded by the love of family and friends, they step into a lifetime together, hand in hand and heart to heart.

On October 03, 2026, they become one. ♡
